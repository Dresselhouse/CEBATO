Data Source

These speeches were published by the Bank for International Settlements (BIS), an international financial Institution owned by central banks.

The BIS is a forum for discussion, policy analysis and knowledge-sharing among central banks and within the international financial and supervisory community.

Since 1997 over 17.000 speeches by central bankers all over the world have been published.

The speches can be browsed and downloaded at

https://www.bis.org/list/cbspeeches/
